## Overview
- High Speed Brightfield and Fluorescence Slide Scanner.
- Able to perform monochrome fluorescence or colour imaging of sections in a fully automated manner. 
- Fluorescence Specifications:
    - 7 high powered LED light source [Zeiss Colibri](EN_product-info_Colibri-7_rel1-0.pdf) for fluorescence excitation:
        - 385nm, 430nm, 475nm, 555nm, 590nm, 630nm, and 735nm 
    - Pentaband emission filters to allow high speed imaging using a sCMOS monochrome camera.
