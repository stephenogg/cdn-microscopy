## Overview
The UltraMicroscope Blaze is a light sheet microscope designed for subcellular 3D imaging of large and multiple samples. A standard sample chamber accommodates multiple rodent organs/organoids, while the XXL upgrade expands capacity for human kidney or whole adult mouse models. MACS iQ View-3D Large Volume, a software package for image processing, is used for data interrogation.
